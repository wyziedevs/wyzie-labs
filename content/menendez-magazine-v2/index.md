## Overview

The MenMag v2.0 is an update to the original MenMag v1.0, the first 3D-printable Glock magazines. The v2.0 solves the biggest issues with the original: difficult printing, limited lifespan, nosediving, tight magwell fitment, and inconsistency between prints. Every part was redesigned from scratch (no v1.0 parts are compatible).

In dry-cycle testing, magazines lasted over 1,000 rounds without the body wearing out, a massive improvement over the v1.0. Live-fire testing showed perfect reliability across hundreds of rounds in multiple applications.

## Print Settings

- **Nozzle:** 0.4 mm (mandatory)
- **Layer Height:** 0.15 mm (strongly recommended, feed lips depend on it)
- **Temp:** 230 °C nozzle / 60 °C bed
- **Infill:** 100%
- **Walls:** 8 recommended (makes the body essentially all walls, reduces swelling when loaded)
- **Supports:** None on magazine body, baseplate, or locking tab. Optional full supports on follower only.
- **Cooling Fan:** Off by default, autocooling 35–60% min/max, 80% bridges.
- **Bed Adhesion:** Glue stick or hairspray.

All STL files are pre-scaled and pre-oriented. Print as-is.
